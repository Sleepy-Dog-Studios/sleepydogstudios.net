About The Obsessive Shadow:

https://discord.com/invite/2Aa9BR2YFW

The Obsessive Shadow is an Indie VR Survival horror game about fending off a home intruder that you can't exactly identify... Keep yourself safe with nothing but a flashlight and your own eyes...

You're going about your business winding down at home for the night when suddenly you see a very strange emergency broadcast come on the T.V. followed by a knock on the window...

Watch your windows, something is close by and watching you...

your flashlight is your best friend

Keep things quiet to avoid angering him...

Check under the bed, something is reaching for you!
