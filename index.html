ABOUT THE STUDIO

Key features of our studio

Games

We make video games! We are a team of self-taught game developers! We love making games and bringing entertainment to your screens.

Passion

We are all very passionate about what we do! We work hard and always prioritize making something the best we can rather than rushing to release a product.

VR

At Sleepy Dog Studios, we work hard to bring you VR content! We are all VR enthusiasts, so it isn't hard to find drive to make these ambitious projects.

Location

We are located all over the United States coming from different backgrounds. This helps us bring inclusion to our games!
